Design and Analysis of Algortihms

The Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) course focuses on creating efficient solutions for computational problems through well-structured algorithms. It explores diverse strategies for designing algorithms and analyzing their performance in terms of time and space complexities.

ABOUT THE COURSE

This course equips students with the knowledge to design, implement, and analyze algorithms effectively. It emphasizes practical problem-solving techniques and theoretical foundations to understand computational challenges.
